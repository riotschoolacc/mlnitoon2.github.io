Worko: Reporting Platform

Worko connects students and teachers for easy communication. Submit reports, track progress, and collaborate directly with your educators.

How to Use Worko

  1. Visit the Site: Go to Worko Homepage.
  2. Sign Up: New users register by entering your name, email, and password.
  3. Sign In: Returning users log in with your email and password.
  4. Student Dashboard: Submit reports about classmates that are misbehaving.
  5. Teacher Dashboard: Manage reports, generate class codes, and communicate with students.
